How to Make a Call on Any Android Phone

Step 1:

Find the green phone icon on your screen. It looks like a telephone.

Step 2:

Tap the green phone icon once with your finger. This opens the phone app.

Step 3:

Tap the button that says "Keypad" or shows numbers at the bottom.

Step 4:

Use your finger to tap the numbers of the phone number you want to call.

Step 5:

After typing the number, tap the big green phone button to start the call.

Step 6:

To finish the call, tap the red phone button that looks like a phone hanging up.

If you want to call someone saved in your contacts, tap "Contacts" instead of "Keypad," find their name, then tap it and the green phone button.
